> HQ : HQ is responsible of leading their respective unit. They also have the task to manage the National Space Research & Forces Corps. They are the most professional and the highest
ranked members of the entire corps.

> SPU : SPU is responsible of managing the National Space Research & Forces Corps. They oversee the corps and administrate it by working with the leaders of it.

> SIU : SIU is responsible in protecting the National Space Research & Forces Corps. They are ensuring its security as well as the security of its members. Then, they are to ensure the
worldwide cyber security. Ex: if someone manages to hack into NSRFC infrastructures, they are to take care of the situation. Lastly, they are to conduct spying operations with or without 
secret agents.

> SEU : SEU is reponsible in conducting 0 day space operations. Ex: Exploring a newly discovered galaxy that nobody ever explored. They are to be extremely vigilant as their life is 
constantly in danger.

> SD : SD is responsible in researching space phenomen. They are doing everything related to sciences, such as physics, chemistry, maths, etc...

> MD : MD is responsible in ensuring stable health in every members of the National Space Research & Forces Corps. They do surgeries, interventions, etc...

> SAT : SAT is responsible in analysing everything related to space and/or anything suspicious found on land. They obtain their subjects after explorations.

> SOU : SOU is responsible in conducting space operations. They are to attack, invade, or just pacifically intervent in newly discovered galaxies/planets, already explored by SEU before.

> PD : PD is responsible in ensuring the protection in space and on land of everyone within the corps. They are armed and defends our structure.

> NSTU : NSTU is in charge of conducting the trainings for the ones who are willing to enter a space regiment. The trainees must pass National Land Trainings before entering the 
National Space Trainings program. Only the most skilled of them all will be accepted into this aformentioned program.

> NLTU : NLTU is in charge of conducting the trainings for the newly incomers within the National Space Research & Forces Corps. They are to ensure every new personnel is highly skilled and 
that they deserve to join our corps.
